Instructions: Deception is the act of hiding the truth. For this task, you will be asked to guess the intentions of a robot. The robot's current position and direction is shown by a golden arrow. If the robot has changed its position, then you will be shown golden line segments that illustrate the robot's history. Using the robot's past position, directions, and pattern of movement, you will try to guess if the robot is heading to either the red or blue locations. Once you click the next button, you will not be able to return to the previous set of questions. You will be asked to guess the robot's intentions and you will be asked what information was the most important when you made your guess. Keep in mind that if you are shown a blue or red path, then the red path is the best path to reach the red location and the blue path is the best path to reach the blue location.

Important Definitions:

Trend: The general trend of the data over time. For example, are the x and y coordinates of the robot's position approaching a certain point?

Direction: The orientation of the robot. For example, if a line were drawn from the tail end of the golden arrow to the head of the arrow, where is this line pointing?
